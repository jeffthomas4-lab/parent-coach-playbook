# Facebook group distribution stage

**Status:** inert drafts only. No account creation. No auto-posting. A human pastes every draft at the HUMAN GATE after checking the specific group's rules and the live conversation.

This lane is for answering an existing parent question helpfully, not dropping unsolicited links, impersonating a community member, scraping groups, joining groups automatically, or manufacturing engagement. Jeff chooses the group and thread, edits the copy in his voice, and decides whether a link is allowed. If the answer is not useful without the link, do not post it.

### Facebook Draft 1
- **Group fit:** A parent is asking what to say the night before tryouts; group rules permit relevant first-party resources.
- **Copy:** Keep tonight boring. Pack the bag, eat the normal dinner, and skip the last-minute speech. The useful question is not “Are you ready?” but “What time do we need to leave?” I wrote down the short version of what helps and what tends to add pressure.

- **Group fit:** A parent is asking how to respond immediately after a bad game; no active crisis or safeguarding concern.
- **Copy:** The first job is not analysis. Give them water, get moving, and let the nervous system come down. If they want to talk, listen before you coach. Here is the 90-second version I use with sports parents.
- **Link:** https://parentcoachdesk.com/drive-home/the-90-second-rule/?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=group-help-drive-home
