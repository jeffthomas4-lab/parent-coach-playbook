## What was drafted

**`src/content/news/nfhs-track-field-throwing-warmup-safety-2027.md`** (draft: true)
NFHS approved a rule July 10, 2026: shot put and discus warmup throws can no longer be made backward or over the head from the toe board — all warmups must now face the sector from inside the circle, matching competition form. Takes effect with the 2027 outdoor season, not this spring. Source: NFHS. Not covered anywhere on the site.

**`src/content/news/youth-soccer-age-cutoff-shifts-to-school-year-2026.md`** (draft: true)
US Youth Soccer, AYSO, and US Club Soccer are replacing the January–December birth-year cutoff with an August–July school-year cutoff, starting with the 2026-27 season on August 1, 2026. Kids born August–December are most likely to land in a new age group. Decision itself dates to June 2025 (site never covered it), but the effective date is now inside the 14-day-relevant window for parent action: fall registration is open now and may already use the new cutoff. Flagging the judgment call: this isn't "new news," it's an imminent deadline that finally warranted a post.
