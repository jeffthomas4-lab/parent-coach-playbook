## What was drafted

**`src/content/news/usssa-grade-exception-limit-2026.md`** (draft: true)
Starting August 12, 2026, USSSA caps every travel baseball team at three grade-exception players, the kids rostered by school grade instead of strict age. There was no cap before. Individual events (showcases) are exempt. Parent action: if your kid holds a roster spot on a grade exception and the team already carries three, that spot is now at risk, so confirm status with the team manager before the deadline sorts it out. Source: USSSA Baseball National By-Laws & Rules (primary rulebook, Rule 3.00 header), confirmed verbatim this pass. Not covered anywhere on the site. This closes the unverified flag carried in the 2026-07-07 and 2026-07-14 reports.
