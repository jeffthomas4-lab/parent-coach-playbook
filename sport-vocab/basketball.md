# Basketball — sport vocabulary

The court has its own geography. Position names are well-defined. Use them.

## The court

- Correct: court, baseline (or end line), sideline, half-court line, three-point line, free-throw line, free-throw lane (the "key" or "paint"), the lane, the paint, top of the key, elbow, wing, corner, block, post, low post, high post, hoop, rim, backboard, net
- Wrong for this sport: pitch, diamond, gridiron, rink, end zone

## Players and positions

- Point guard (PG, "the 1"), shooting guard (SG, "the 2"), small forward (SF, "the 3"), power forward (PF, "the 4"), center (C, "the 5")
- The kid with the ball is the ball-handler. On defense, the player on the ball is the on-ball defender; off the ball is help defense.
- Wrong for this sport: pitcher, striker, midfielder, lineman, libero, setter, goalie

## Action verbs

- Dribble, pass, shoot, finish, lay it in (layup), dunk, post up, cut, drive, pull up, step back, screen (or "set a screen" or "ball screen"), roll, pop, fade, box out, rebound, block, steal, switch, hedge, recover, close out, contest
- Pass types: bounce pass, chest pass, overhead pass, outlet pass, skip pass
- Wrong for this sport: pitch, kick (kicking the ball is a violation), spike, dig, snap, tackle, body-check, cradle

## Scoring

- A team scores a basket worth 2 points (inside the arc) or 3 points (beyond the three-point line), or 1 point per made free throw.
- Made shot, hit a shot, sank it, knocked it down, drained it (slang — sparingly)
- Wrong for this sport: run, goal, touchdown, kill. ("Field goal" is technically used in basketball box scores to mean any non-free-throw made shot, but in beginner-parent prose, write "basket" or "shot." A "field goal" sounds like football to a parent.)

## Game structure

- Quarter (NBA: 12 minutes; high school: 8 minutes; youth varies — cite the league), half, halftime, overtime, possession, shot clock (varies — high school and youth often have no shot clock), full timeout, 30-second timeout
- Wrong for this sport: inning, period (period is for hockey, not basketball, despite some leagues using it), set, match, down

## Equipment

- Basketball (size 5 youth, size 6 women's/girls' middle school, size 7 men's/boys' high school+), basketball shoes, mouthguard (sometimes required)
- Wrong for this sport: bat, glove, stick, helmet, pads, cleats

## Cross-sport pitfalls (most common slips)

- "Scored a goal" — wrong. Scored a basket, made the shot, hit a 3.
- "Kicked the ball" — kicking is a violation. Players don't kick the ball deliberately.
- "The runner" — basketball doesn't have runners. The ball-handler, the cutter, the player driving.
- "Net" is fine for the basket's net. "Hit nothing but net" is the swish phrase, but write "swish" or "all net" if using the slang.
- "Field goal" reads as football to a parent. Use "basket" or "shot."
