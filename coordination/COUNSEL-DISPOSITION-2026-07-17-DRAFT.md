## Purpose

The `open_risk_decision` release gate requires that every currently open stop-ship risk be explicitly accepted, closed, or accepted-with-condition by counsel and/or the policy owner (Jeff), in writing, before production readiness advances. This worksheet enumerates every open risk this session could identify from the counsel packet, the deployment-scope incident record, the deliberately-disabled feature list, and the current release-evidence gate states. It does not decide anything. The **Decision**, **Owner**, and **Date** columns are intentionally blank for counsel/Jeff to complete.

This worksheet is an engineering evidence compilation, not legal advice and not a compliance certification.

## How to complete this worksheet

For each row, enter in **Decision**: `accept` (risk acknowledged, no further action required before launch), `close` (risk resolved, cite the closing evidence), or `condition` (accepted only if a stated condition is met — write the condition in the Decision cell). Enter the responsible **Owner** and the **Date** decided. Once every row has a Decision, Owner, and Date, this worksheet and counsel's written disposition on the review packet together satisfy the `open_risk_decision` gate.
