# Editorial Content Lifecycle

This is the governed local operating contract for Parent Coach Desk's authority and acquisition engine. It is intentionally provider-neutral: no automatic publishing, deletion, redirects, affiliate changes, or production database writes are permitted.

## Flow

Demand signal → score → classify intent and content type → existence/cannibalization check → structured brief → source registry → outline → draft → claim validation → editorial review → SEO/AI review → relationship map → monetization/disclosure classification → human approval → publish → indexing/distribution → performance monitoring → refresh, consolidate, or retire.

Signals include search and no-result logs, Search Console, camp and age/season gaps, affiliate gaps, newsletter/support questions, corrections, competitor and emerging-trend review. Raw child or family identifying data never enters the public corpus.

## Evidence gates

Every new or materially changed item needs an intent statement, reviewed sources, claim-level validation, safety/IP/sensitivity review, editorial and SEO review, internal links/knowledge-graph relationships, monetization classification, and explicit Jeff approval. The existing corpus is a legacy baseline until these artifacts are recorded; presence of frontmatter or a green build is not proof of evidence completeness.

## Maintenance

Refresh at 18 months or earlier when facts, seasons, products, or policy change. Fact-check dates and source references are required. Monitor impressions, clicks, conversions, affiliate availability, corrections, and support feedback. Consolidation and retirement require an incoming-link audit and human supersession decision; automation may propose but never execute either action.

## Boundary

Lifecycle states, evidence gates, source quality, QA, and maintenance policy are reusable Forge OS. PCD-specific collections, parent-coaching taxonomy, routes, and affiliate mappings remain in this repository. The deterministic snapshot at `reports/editorial/lifecycle.json` is an honest baseline and not a production approval record.
