## Slug naming convention

`{sport}-{item}-{variant}` lowercase, hyphenated.

Examples: `baseball-glove-9in-youth`, `soccer-ball-size4`, `lacrosse-stick-attack-mens`, `multi-sport-cones-4in`.

When the same item works across sports (cones, water bottles, first-aid kit), use the `multi-sport` prefix and reference the same `/go/` slug from every sport guide.

## Compliance

Every gear card on the site shows the Amazon Associates disclosure: *"As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases."* Inline beneath each card.

Each guide that contains affiliate cards also has a top-of-page disclosure right under the lede, before the first ## section heading.

Affiliate links use `rel="sponsored nofollow noopener"` per Amazon and Google best practice.

Gear cards do not display fixed prices (Amazon Operating Agreement prohibits price display outside the Product Advertising API or live SiteStripe widgets). The category cost ranges in the guide prose are editorial commentary, not price quotes.

The `/disclosure/` page contains the required Amazon Operating Agreement language: site name + "is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program..." + "As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases."
