## Pillar 6 detail (Accessibility) — 2026-07-07 re-verification

**Contrast math (WCAG relative-luminance formula, computed by hand this session, not approximated):** `rust_old = #C5713D` on `paper = #FAF6EE` is 3.41:1 (fails 4.5:1 for normal text). `rust_new = #9E5228` on `paper` is 5.45:1 (passes). `rust_new` on `paper-warm = #F2EAD9` is 4.80:1 (passes; `rust_old` there was 3.00:1, fail). `ink-soft = #5F5448` on `paper` is 6.98:1 (already passing, no change needed). `#5C7459` (the site's status-green, used for success messages) on `paper` is 4.77:1 (already passing). One nuance the interrupted session's token swap did not account for: on the dark hero sections (`background: #2D2520`), `rust_old` was already only 4.01:1 — passing only because those instances are large text (≥24px) against the 3:1 large-text floor. `rust_new` on that same dark background computes to 2.51:1, which would have failed even the large-text floor. Three homepage headline `<em>` spans (`index.astro` lines ~360/371/382) sit at `text-xl` (20px) with `font-weight: 500`, which is normal text under WCAG (not "large," since large requires 24px regular or ~18.7px bold) — these were failing at 4.01:1 even before this session and are not fixable by darkening rust further without also failing the large-text instances, so they were switched to `#D4AB6A` (the site's existing "trophy" dark-background accent, already used for the eyebrow label directly above each of these three headlines) — 6.77:1 on ink, passes. The two h1/h2-level uses of rust-on-dark (`index.astro` ~341, `parent-coach.astro` ~50) are true large text and were left on `rust_old` at 4.01:1 (passes 3:1), since swapping to `rust_new` would have dropped them to 2.51:1 and failed. The button at `index.astro` ~347 (`.btn`, 12px paper-on-rust) was failing at 3.41:1 and is fixed to `rust_new` (5.45:1).

**Every file where a hardcoded `#C5713D` text color was changed to the compliant value:** `src/components/NewsletterSignup.astro`, `src/pages/decisions/[slug].astro` (×2), `src/pages/youth-sports-pendulum.astro` (×2), `src/pages/burnout-check.astro`, `src/pages/admin/link-health/index.astro` (×2), `src/pages/admin/editorial/index.astro`, `src/pages/admin/camps/[id].astro`, `src/pages/camps/submit.astro` (×3, JS-set), `src/pages/camps/suggest.astro` (×2, JS-set), `src/pages/camps/[slug].astro` (×4, JS-set), `src/pages/index.astro` (button background + 3 headline `<em>` spans switched to trophy instead, per above).
