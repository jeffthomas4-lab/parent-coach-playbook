Third-and-short in 7v7 lives or dies on the quick out. This drill gets the receiver to the sticks and the QB to make a fast read.

**Equipment needed:** Two cones marking the line and the sticks (5 yards apart), a football, one receiver, one quarterback, one defender (optional).

**Setup:** Cones set the route. The receiver runs from line to sticks, plants, and turns to the sideline.

**How to run it:**

1. Receiver releases on the snap. Six hard steps to the sticks cone.
2. On the sixth step, plant and turn out, hands ready.
3. QB takes a three-step drop and throws to where the receiver is going, not where they are.
4. Catch should happen at or just past the sticks marker. Six reps.

**What to look for:** A clean break, no rounding the corner. The receiver should snap their head around at the same time they plant. The ball should arrive on the outside shoulder, away from where a defender would close from inside.

**Variation:** With a defender, the receiver works on selling the inside before breaking out. Without one, focus on timing.
