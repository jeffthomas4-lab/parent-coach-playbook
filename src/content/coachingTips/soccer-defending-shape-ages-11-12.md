Defensive shape is a line. This drill teaches defenders to stay in a line while marking attackers and closing down the ball.

**Equipment needed:** One soccer ball, cones to mark a center line and attacking zone, 8 kids (4 attackers, 3 defenders, 1 keeper).

**Setup:** Mark a 40-by-30-yard zone. Three defenders stand in a line about 20 yards from goal. Four attackers spread across the field about 35 yards from goal.

**How to run it:**

1. Attackers pass and advance toward the defending line.
2. Defenders stay in line, jockeying and closing down.
3. Attackers try to break the line or create a clear shot.
4. If defenders win the ball, they clear it up field and reset.
5. Play 10 attacking sequences.
