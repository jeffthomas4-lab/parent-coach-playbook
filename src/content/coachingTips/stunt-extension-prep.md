Extension is the highest standard stunt. Bases get the position right or it collapses.

**Equipment needed:** Mat space, two bases.

**Setup:** Both bases facing each other, slightly closer than shoulder-width.

**How to run it:**

1. Bases dip into a quarter squat.
2. Both extend up, arms straight overhead.
3. Cup the hands as if holding a flyer's foot — one base's outside hand under the heel, inside hand under the ball of the foot. Other base mirrors.
4. Hold ten seconds. Repeat five times.

**What to look for:** Arms LOCKED straight. Bent elbows can't hold an extension. Feet shoulder-width apart, weight on the balls of the feet.

**Variation:** Once the position is solid, add a flyer in stages — first just standing in the bases' hands at chest height, then progress to shoulder height, then full extension.
