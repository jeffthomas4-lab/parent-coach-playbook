Bases who don't match stances don't stunt safely. The setup is the foundation.

**Equipment needed:** Mat.

**Setup:** Two bases facing each other, about three feet apart.

**How to run it:**

1. Both bend the knees into a quarter squat.
2. Inside foot of each base points toward the other base's inside foot.
3. Hands meet at thigh level, palms up, forming a "cup" for the flyer's foot.
4. Hold for five seconds. Check both bases are mirrored.
5. Reset and repeat ten times.

**What to look for:** Symmetry. Both bases at the same depth of squat. Both hands at the same height. If one base is higher, the stunt will tilt.

**Variation:** Have a coach call "set" and time how fast both bases lock into matched position. Three seconds is the target.
