headline: "USSSA travel baseball caps grade-exception players at 3 per team on August 12"
summary: "Starting August 12, 2026, USSSA limits every travel baseball team to three grade-exception players: the kids rostered by school grade instead of strict age. There was no cap before. If your kid plays on a grade exception and the team already carries three, they could lose the roster spot right as fall ball opens. Individual events like showcases are exempt, no limit there. Ask your team manager where your kid stands before the deadline decides it for you."
