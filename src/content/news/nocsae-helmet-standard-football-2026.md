headline: "Pop Warner and most youth football programs now require NOCSAE 2024 certified helmets"
summary: "Pop Warner and the majority of affiliated youth football leagues began enforcing the updated NOCSAE ND002 standard this fall. Helmets certified to the older ND002-13m20 standard are no longer permitted in play. If your child's helmet was purchased before 2023, check the certification sticker inside the shell. A sticker reading ND002-13m20 means it will not pass inspection. Riddell, Schutt, and Xenith have all released recertified youth models; most retail for $80 to $140."
