Softball's first season comes with a question no other sport asks at signup: softball league or baseball league? Around the country, and especially in towns where the fastpitch program is small, plenty of girls start in baseball, and some regions just call the whole sport fastpitch. Here's the honest map.

**Under 9, the leagues are interchangeable.** Tee-ball and coach-pitch are the same game with the same skills regardless of which sign-up form you used. If the baseball league has better fields, better coaches, or her best friend, [it's the better choice](/drive-there/when-softball-splits-from-baseball/), full stop. The overhand throw, the swing, the base running all transfer completely when she switches later.

**The gear takes one trip.** A glove she can actually close, a helmet with a facemask, which is standard in softball even where baseball skips it, rubber cleats, and a bat. About $100, and the [softball gear guide](/what-to-buy/softball/) has picks by age with the store tests. The fielder's mask question arrives around 8U-10U; most infield coaches now want one, and we're for it.

**Games look like baseball with better energy.** Same diamond, shorter base paths, an 11-inch ball at the young ages. Softball dugouts chant. This is a defining feature of the sport, it never stops, and you will know every cheer by July whether you want to or not.

**Pitching is the sport's big fork, and it's not a year-one decision.** Fastpitch's windmill motion is a taught skill, not a playground one, which is why pitching defines softball budgets later. In year one, nobody pitches; coach-pitch and machine-pitch carry the games. If she falls for the sport, [the pitching decision](/drive-there/the-pitching-circle-decision/) arrives around 9 or 10, and fall ball is the perfect low-stakes place to try it.

**The travel pitch will find you early.** Travel fastpitch orgs recruit at 8U and 9U now, usually at the all-star tournament in front of the families who just had a good June. The development case for travel at that age is thin and the costs aren't; we put [the real numbers here](/drive-there/how-much-does-travel-softball-cost/). There's no falling behind at eight.

**What a good first season looks like:** she swings without being talked into it, she has a position she claims even if the coach rotates everyone, she's learned one chant you now hear in your sleep, and she asks about fall ball. That's the whole scoreboard.
