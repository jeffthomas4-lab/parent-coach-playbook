## Indexing and sitemap

Sitemap is clean. https://parentcoachdesk.com/sitemap.xml is a sitemap index, submitted June 11, last read June 21, status Success, 3,261 pages discovered. No processing problems.

Coverage has no red errors. 390 pages indexed, 2,820 not indexed across 8 reasons. The two big buckets are "Discovered, currently not indexed" (1,539) and "Crawled, currently not indexed" (1,105). That is normal for a brand-new site with 3,261 pages. Google found them, has not gotten around to indexing most of them yet. Time and internal links fix that, not a settings change.

The smaller buckets are not worth action this week: 93 alternate pages with canonical (expected), 39 duplicate-canonical, 30 blocked by robots.txt, 15 not found 404, 2 redirects, 1 soft 404. Nothing new or broken. The 15 404s would be worth a glance if that number grows.
